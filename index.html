The Issues

01

Housing That's Actually Affordable

Too many residents are being priced out of the neighborhoods they built. Jackson will push for [specific policy], protect renters from [specific issue], and ensure development serves the community — not just developers.

  • Expand affordable housing stock
  • Tenant protections and rent stabilization
  • Community land trusts
02

Public Safety Done Right

Safety means more than policing. Jackson believes in a whole-community approach — mental health response teams, youth programs, lit streets, and public spaces everyone actually wants to be in.

  • Co-responder mental health programs
  • Youth investment and after-school funding
  • Community-led violence prevention
03

A Greener, Cleaner Borough

Climate change isn't coming — it's here. Jackson will fight for green infrastructure, clean energy transition, and environmental justice for the neighborhoods that have borne the biggest burden.

  • Green infrastructure investment
  • Emissions reduction targets
  • Environmental justice for frontline communities
04

An Economy That Works For Everyone

Small businesses are the backbone of our district. Jackson will champion local hiring, support small business owners, and make sure economic growth benefits longtime residents — not just newcomers.

  • Local hiring requirements on public contracts
  • Small business support and grants
  • Anti-displacement policies
